On Building Things

November 10, 2025

There's something deeply satisfying about building things from scratch. Whether it's code, furniture, or even a simple website, the process of taking an idea and turning it into something real is incredibly rewarding.

The Joy of Creation

When you build something yourself, you understand it completely. Every decision, every trade-off, every little detail is yours. You know why things are the way they are because you made them that way.

This understanding creates a connection to your work that's hard to achieve any other way.

Starting Simple

I've found that the best projects often start with the simplest possible version. Add complexity only when you need it, not because you think you might need it someday.

This website is a perfect example - just HTML and CSS, nothing more complicated than necessary.
